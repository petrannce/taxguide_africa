Frequently Asked Questions

Common Questions
Answered

01 What types of organizations does TaxGuide Africa LLP serve?
We serve a wide spectrum of organizations — multinational corporations, SMEs, start-ups, NGOs, government agencies, financial institutions, and individual investors. Our five practice areas are designed to address the needs of virtually any organization operating across Africa.
02 How quickly can I expect a response to my inquiry?
We aim to respond to all inquiries within one business day. For urgent matters, call us directly on +254 711 355 015 or message us on WhatsApp for near-immediate assistance.
03 Do you provide services outside Kenya?
Yes. TaxGuide Africa LLP provides cross-border advisory services across multiple African jurisdictions — including international tax planning, transfer pricing, cross-border legal structuring, and multinational financial advisory support.
04 How is client confidentiality protected?
Confidentiality is a non-negotiable core value. All client information is handled under strict professional and ethical obligations. We maintain rigorous data protection standards and never share client information with third parties without explicit consent.
05 Can I have an initial consultation before committing?
Absolutely. We offer initial consultations to understand your organization's challenges and explore how we can best support you — with no obligation. Use the form above, call us, or send a WhatsApp message to arrange yours.
06 What information should I prepare before contacting you?
A brief description of your organization, the nature of your challenge or inquiry, and any relevant timeline or urgency is helpful. The more context you provide, the faster we can connect you with the right specialist. You are not required to have everything figured out before reaching out.
